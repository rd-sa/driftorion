Official Communities

Rookies

A welcoming space for new drifters, offering structured guidance and coaching to help you grow fast and confidently.

The Clubhouse

A dynamic gathering spot where drifters come together for casual events, friendly competition, and shared creativity.

Freerunners

A dedicated zone for movement lovers to train, explore, and enjoy the thrill of parkour and climbing in a supportive environment.

Passers Club

A moderated fleet focused on precision passing, strong coordination, and elevating team play with every game.

Competitive

Designed for ambitious drifters seeking ranked play, skill development, and a path to victory through serious competition.

Golf/Club Community

Join the Orion Drift Golf Community, where golf enthusiasts come together to enjoy every aspect of the club district.

Girls Who Drift

An inclusive space where girls and non-binary players can connect, drift, and enjoy the game in a supportive environment.

Grown Ups Club

A community for adult gamers looking for a chill, skill-focused atmosphere among other mature players.

Find Your Crew

Each community has its own Discord channel or server where you can connect with like-minded drifters, play in organized events, and enjoy the orion together. Join multiple communities to experience all that Orion Drift has to offer!
