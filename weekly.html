Weekly Challenges

Week 1: Precision Wall Run

Chain together five wall‐runs without touching the ground. Time yourself and try to beat your personal best!

Week 2: Speed Vault Circuit

Complete the vault circuit in under 45 seconds. Focus on fluidity and minimal stops.

Week 3: Long Distance Leap

Make the longest continuous leap across labeled platforms. Record the distance.

Video credit: Orion Drift Official YouTube

Week 4: Ceiling Cruise

Run inverted along the ceiling for at least 12 seconds without falling.

Video credit: Orion Drift Official YouTube

Week 5: Freestyle Showcase

Submit your most creative parkour flow combining at least five different moves.

Video credit: Orion Drift Official YouTube
